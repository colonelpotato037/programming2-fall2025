Quon-style Goulash with Dragon Cutlet

Description

Recipe of a special goulash from Quon with Dragon cutlet on top. This is a custom recipe. Every family in Quon has their own preferenced when making Goulash. They also never put a dragon cutlet on top before. That is an original idea of a Draking Shipâ€™s Chef.

Dragon Cutlet

Ingredients

  • Dragon red meat 200g
  • 1 egg
  • Breadcrumbs 1 cup
  • Finely grated cheese 4 tbsp
  • Dragon fat (or dragon cooking oil) as needed
  • Butter 15 g
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Flour as needed

Steps

  1. Pound the meat while pulling towards yourself to thin and scratch it to desired size and thickness.
  2. Season the meat with salt and pepper.
  3. Add grated cheese to finely crushed bread crumbs and mix well. Dust dragon meat with flour, dip in beaten egg, then cover in breadcrumb mixture to form breading. Lightly press down the exterior.
  4. Melt enough dragon fat and butter in a frying pan to fill about five mm up the side and heat. Fry the meat until golden brown on one side.
  5. Flip and cook the other side, drain, and serve.

Quon-style Goulash

Ingredients

  • Dragon Meat (at least two different cuts/varieties) 200g
  • 1 Onion
  • Salt 1-2 Tsp
  • Caraway seed (or cumin) 1/2 tbsp
  • Paprika 2-3 tbsp
  • Red bell pepper 1
  • Dragon fat 50 g
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Carrot 1 bunch
  • Tomato 1
  • Celery leaves 1 stick worth
  • Potato 300 g
  • Bacon (if desired) 50 g

Steps

  1. Cut meat into bite-sized pieces. Mince onion and garlic, and chop the carrot, tomato, bell pepper, and potato into one cm pieces. Tie the celery leaves with a string.
  2. Melt dragon fat in a cauldron or pot and cook onions until golden brown (along with bacon if using). Add caraway and garlic and lightly saute.
  3. Add dragon meat and season with 1-2 tsp of salt. Sprinkle generously with paprika to coat, then close the lid.
  4. Once the meat has cooked partway, add all vegetables excluding the potato and carrot and add enough water to cover. Simmer for two hours (gradually add more water if too much evaporates).
  5. Lastly, add potato and carrot to the pot and simmer until tender. Remove the celery leaves and its done. Put the slices of dragon cutlet on top and enjoy.
