Pressed Dragon Liver Confit

description

This is a dish made by Vannie for Mika as a late night snack, though every crew ends up taking a bite from it. It’s a pretty simple and straightforward recipe. Even Mika is surprised by how simply stacking ingredients on top of each other could end up taste this good.

Ingredients

  • Dragon liver: 300 gram
  • Potato: 3 ~ 4
  • Pickled cabbage: as needed
  • Salt: to taste
  • Pepper: to taste
  • Garlic: 1 ~ 2 cloves
  • Dragon Fat: Enough to submerge liver

Steps

  1. Season dragon liver with salt, pepper, and minced garlic. Feel free to add herbs and spices.
  2. To make the confit, cover the liver with dragon fat in a large pot and simmer at about 175° Fahrenheit for 1.5 – 2 hours.
  3. Mash potatoes and season with salt, pepper, and a dash of confit fat. Mix together well.
  4. Line the inside of a mold with pickled cabbage, then layer the mashed potato mixture and sliced liver confit, pressing each layer to remove any air pockets.
  5. Fold pickled cabbage over the top of the ingredients and press down firmly (traditionally, a weight is used to press the loaf for 2-3 hours).
  6. Slide a knife along the edge of the mold and gently remove the terrine. Enjoy.
