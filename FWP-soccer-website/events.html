Senior Night

After a contested first half, the Colonels entered into halftime against Disney 2 with a score of 1-1 after a stunning strike from Johnny Backx with less than a minuete to go in the half. The colonels came out to a blazing start to the 2nd half, putting up three goals to make the score 4-1 with 20 minuets to go. but the story doesnt end there! After a PK and a couple of well placed shots, the score had even out at 4-4 with less than five minuetes left. with time of the essance, the colonels kicked into overdrive, and Senior Heath Albert got in the perfect postion for an open net, striking home the game winning goal, ending senior night with a 5-4 victory.

Regionals

After a sound 9-0 mercy rule victory over Holy Trinity, the Colonels spirits ran high. this attitude carried them through the week as they prepared to face Marine Leadership Academy. When the day of the game finally came. The Colonels were ready. An unfortunate mistake put MLA up 1-0, but that lead did not last, as the Johnny Backx quickly struck back, equalizing at 1-1. while it looked like smooth sailing, the MLA keeper wouldnt give an inch, and a PK late in the half brought the halftime score to 2-1, MLA. But the Colonels kept up the pressure. After a beutiful PK from Oberon Goldman, the score had evened out at 2-2. The Colonels continued thier pressure, and after a wonderfull setup from Raffaelo Franco, Johnny Backx made a wonderfull flick to Brandon Wollin who buried the shot. With Brandons GWG, the Colonels took home another regional title have a hardfought 3-2 victory over Marine Leadership Academy.
